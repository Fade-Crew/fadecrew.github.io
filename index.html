Grid ⚡

Grid is basically Godot, but we've tweaked it (a lot) to see what happens when you try new ideas without being afraid to break things. It's built on top of the amazing Godot Engine—we're not trying to replace it, just exploring what else is possible.

We made this because sometimes the best tool is the one that works exactly how you need it to. Grid is our playground for testing cleaner workflows, better UX, and tools that actually make sense when you're deep in a project at 2am.

A football experience that pushes far past the usual boundaries of the genre. Built to feel authentic, tactical, and alive in ways traditional football games don't even attempt.
